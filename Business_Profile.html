Project Profile: Webecon Animated Icons

Product Overview

Webecon is a professional iconography engine hosted at webecon.adityadivte.com. It provides over 1,000+ premium animated SVG icons and native SDKs. Our core philosophy is to empower the global developer community by providing high-end UI assets 100% free for commercial and personal use.

Usage & Open Access

The software and assets are distributed under the Webecon Open-Brand License, allowing any developer, startup, or corporation to integrate our icons into their commercial software without licensing costs.

Community Focused

Free SDKs for React, Flutter, Swift, and 12+ other platforms.

Commercial Freedom

Zero cost for integration into paid apps, websites, and games.

Support & Revenue Model

As an independent production, our project is sustained through the voluntary contributions of the global developer community and professional service requests:

  • Voluntary Developer Support / Contribution Variable
  • Professional Integration Assistance Service Fee
  • Custom Motion Design Services Project-based

Terms & Refund Policy

Service Delivery: Webecon assets are delivered instantly via CDN and open-access downloads. Professional services are delivered via digital communication and asset transfer.

Refund Policy: Voluntary contributions and project support payments are non-refundable as they are intended for the ongoing maintenance and development of the free ecosystem. For custom design services, refunds are managed on a case-by-case basis before project initiation.
